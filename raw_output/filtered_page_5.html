Prospective Tenants Notice:

Please be advised that Western University does not endorse, approve, or confirm the accuracy of any content posted by a landlord or property manager on this listing website, including whether a Landlord has obtained any required Rental Unit License with the City of London. Landlords and property managers are solely responsible for the accuracy of their posted content. However, if a concern is raised to Western University regarding a post, we will review the matter and act accordingly. This may result in a landlord or a post being removed from the website. Prior to entering into a rental agreement, we recommend that prospective tenants should:

  • inspect the rental unit and property in person,
  • research the neighbourhood and public transportation,
  • ask current tenants about the property, the rental unit, and the landlord.
  • verify if this landlord has a Rental Unit License with the City of London. In some specified cases, the unit/house may not require an official rental license with the City of London. More information can be found here: https://london.ca/business-development/business-licences/residential-rental-unit-licences
  • visit the Frequently Asked Questions Pages on our website, and/or on the Landlord and Tenant Board website. You'll find information on roommate, garbage, noise, safety, rent, pets and smoking, landlord/tenant' relationship, etc.
